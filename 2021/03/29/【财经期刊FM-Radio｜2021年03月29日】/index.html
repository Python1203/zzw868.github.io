A reporter from Xinhua News Agency found that inthe current new energy automobile industry, problems such as “productindicators are still dumping customers” and “the market value of acompany is hundreds of billions before selling a car” has gradually becomeprominent, “false propaganda” and “excessive falsehood”.Has become a hidden worry restricting the further healthy development of theindustry.
