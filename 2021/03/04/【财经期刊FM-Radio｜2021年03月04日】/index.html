要闻详情

中国2月财新服务业PMI回落至51.5 继续位于荣枯线上方

China’s Caixin service industryPMI fell to 51.5 in February and remains above the line of prosperity

受国内外疫情反复等影响，服务业修复的边际动能进一步减弱，中国2月财新服务业PMI录得51.5，持平预期，低于前值52。

Affected by the repeated epidemics at homeand abroad, the marginal momentum of service industry restoration has furtherweakened. China’s Caixin service industry PMI recorded 51.5 in February, whichwas unchanged from expectations and was lower than the previous value of 52.

上海发布楼市新政 七条举措进一步加强房地产管理

Shanghai issues new propertymarket policies, seven measures to further strengthen real estate management

实施住房限售。对按照优先购房政策购买的新建商品住房，在购房合同网签备案满5年后方可转让。上海也限售了！新一轮楼市调控来袭。面对楼市“虚火”，广州、深圳之后，上海也终于祭出了限售大招。

Implement housing sales restrictions. Thenewly-built commercial housing purchased in accordance with the prioritypurchase policy can only be transferred after 5 years of signing and filing thehouse purchase contract online. Shanghai is also restricted for sale! A newround of property market regulation is coming. In the face of the “virtualfire” in the property market, after Guangzhou and Shenzhen, Shanghaifinally offered a big move to restrict sales.

“十四五”首份新能源开发征求意见稿发布以补贴换消纳、地方政府主导开发
