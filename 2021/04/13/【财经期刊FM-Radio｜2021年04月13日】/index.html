Ant group applied to be a financial holdingcompany as a whole. The second is to break theinformation monopoly, strictly implement the requirements of the regulations onthe administration of credit reference industry, operate personal creditreference business with a license in accordance with the law, collect and usepersonal information in accordance with the principle of “legality, minimumand necessity”, and ensure the security of personal and nationalinformation. Third, ant group has applied to be a financial holding company asa whole, all institutions engaged in financial activities are included in thefinancial holding company to accept supervision, improve risk isolationmeasures, and standardize related party transactions. Fourth, strictlyimplement the requirements of prudential supervision, improve corporategovernance, seriously rectify illegal credit, insurance, financial managementand other financial activities, and control high leverage and risk contagion.Fifth, control the liquidity risk of important fund products and activelyreduce the balance of yu’ebao.
