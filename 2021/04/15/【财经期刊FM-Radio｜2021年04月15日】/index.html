Guiyang starts three-levelresponse to liquidity risk of housing provident fund: liquidity has beenseriously insufficient

The pharmaceutical industry isthe heavyweight! The National Health Commission announced the launch of specialtreatment action for unreasonable medical examination

行动将严肃查处违反相关法律法规、诊疗技术规范，损害人民群众利益的不合理医疗检查。包括各类影像学检查、实验室检查、病理学检查等。

The action will seriously investigate anddeal with unreasonable medical examination that violates relevant laws andregulations, diagnosis and treatment technical specifications and damages theinterests of the people. Including all kinds of imaging examination, laboratoryexamination, pathological examination, etc.

Net profit doubled year on year?The Q1 revenue and profit of Gree Electric Co., Ltd. dropped sharply comparedwith that before the epidemic
