要闻详情

2021年清明档票房超8.2亿，和观影人次均创同档期新高，《我的姐姐》占半壁江山成票房冠军

In 2021, the boxoffice of Qingming Festival exceeded 820 million, and the number of people watchingthe film reached a new high in the same period. “My sister” accountedfor half of the country and became the box office champion

灯塔专业版数据显示，截至4月5日24时，4月3日至5日2021年清明档总票房超8.2亿，2225万观影人次，票房及观影人次均创清明档期新高。三日内《我的姐姐》合计票房超3.78亿，占比超46%，成为清明档票房冠军。

According to the data of Lighthouseprofessional edition, as of 24:00 on April 5, from April 3 to 5, the total boxoffice of the Qingming Festival in 2021 exceeded 820 million, with 22.25million movie goers.
