证监会：IPO前12个月入股股东均需锁定三年

China SecuritiesRegulatory Commission: Shareholders must lock in for three years in the 12months before IPO

证监会要求发行人清理股份代持，不存在违规持股情况；提交申请前12个月内入股的新增股东都要锁定36个月；加强对入股价格异常、多层嵌套股东的监管，要求穿透核查；压实中介机构责任；形成监管合力。

The China Securities Regulatory Commissionrequires the issuer to clean up the stock holdings, and there is no illegalshareholding; new shareholders who have purchased shares within the 12 monthsbefore submitting the application must be locked for 36 months; strengthen thesupervision of abnormal share prices and multi-level nested shareholders,Require penetrating verification; compact the responsibilities of intermediaryagencies; form a regulatory force.

证监会亮剑“影子股东”、突击入股等IPO乱象

IPO chaos such as”shadow shareholder” and surprise shareholding by the ChinaSecurities Regulatory Commission

指引将需要锁定36个月的“突击入股”时间段由申报前6个月增加到申报前12个月。值得关注的是，指引重点针对“影子股东”现象进行规范，加强对入股交易价格明显异常的自然人股东和多层嵌套机构股东的信息穿透核查。

The guidelines increase the 36-month periodof “unannounced shareholding” from 6 months before the declaration to12 months before the declaration. It is worth noting that the guidelines focuson regulating the phenomenon of “shadow shareholders” and strengthenthe information penetration verification of natural person shareholders andshareholders of multi-level nested institutions whose share transaction pricesare obviously abnormal.

证监会：批准深交所主板和中小板合并

China SecuritiesRegulatory Commission: Approved the merger of the Shenzhen Stock Exchange’smain board and the small and medium-sized board

证监会表示，合并深交所的主板和中小板的安排是“两个统一、四个不变”，即统一业务规则，统一运行监管模式，保持发行上市条件不变，投资者门槛不变，交易机制不变，证券代码及筒称不变。

The China Securities Regulatory Commissionstated that the arrangement for merging the main board and the small and mediumboard of the Shenzhen Stock Exchange is “two unified, four unchanged”, that is,unified business rules, unified operation and supervision model, and maintainsthe same issuance and listing conditions, unchanged investor thresholds, andtransactions The mechanism remains unchanged, and the securities code and shortname remain unchanged.
