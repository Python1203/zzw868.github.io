China’s social financing scale increased by5.17 trillion yuan in January, which is expected to be 4.6 trillion yuan, fromthe previous value of 1.72 trillion yuan.

Office of the State Council:Actively support the listing of Chinese medicine companies to raise bonds andissue bonds

周二，国务院办公厅印发《关于加快中医药特色发展的若干政策措施》的通知称，医疗机构炮制使用的中药饮片、中药制剂实行自主定价，符合条件的按规定纳入医保支付范围。还要发布中医优势病种，鼓励实行中西医同病同效同价。

It is also necessary to publish the dominant diseasesof traditional Chinese medicine, and encourage the implementation of the samedisease with the same effect and price.

深圳：中介不能发高于参考价房源，高价房过户需签风险告知书

Shenzhen: Intermediaries cannotissue houses with a higher price than the reference price. The transfer ofhigh-priced houses requires a risk notice

从即日起，二手房经纪公司不能发布高于成交参考价的房源信息，高于参考价的房源可以过户，但买家必须签一份风险告知书。深圳二手住房成交参考价格原则上一年发布一次，市场波动大时也可能会半年发布一次。商业银行将根据成交参考价格发放二手房贷款。

From now on, second-hand real estatebrokerage companies cannot publish information on housing above the referenceprice of the transaction, and housing above the reference price can betransferred, but the buyer must sign a risk notice. In principle, the referenceprice of second-hand housing transactions in Shenzhen is released once a year,and may be released every six months when the market is volatile. Commercialbanks will issue second-hand housing loans based on the transaction referenceprice.

MSCI季评揭晓 新兴市场指数新纳入美的、金龙鱼、农夫山泉

The top three market capitalizations of theMSCI China All-Share Index include Midea Group, Nongfu Spring and CICC. The topthree new stocks included in the MSCI China A-Share Onshore Index are CICC,Transsion Holdings and Cobos.

Ren Zhengfei: Adhere toGlobalization, Huawei will never sell terminal business

2020年华为的销售收入和利润都实现了正增长。任正非称，“我的信心更大了，而不是更小了。我们有了更多能克服困难的手段。”

In 2020, Huawei’s sales revenue and profitshave achieved positive growth. Ren Zhengfei said, “My confidence isgreater, not smaller. We have more means to overcome difficulties.”

商务部：加大汽车个人消费信贷支持 适当下调首付比例和贷款利率

Ministry of Commerce: Increasingsupport for auto personal consumer credit and appropriately reducing downpayment ratio and loan interest rates

商务部通知鼓励限购地区号牌指标数量配置向新能源汽车倾斜，对无车家庭购置首辆新能源家用汽车给予支持；适当下调汽车首付比例和贷款利率，延长还款期限。

The Ministry of Commerce notified toencourage the allocation of number plates in areas with restricted purchases totilt towards new energy vehicles, and support car-free families to purchase thefirst new energy family car; appropriately lower the car down payment ratio andloan interest rate, and extend the repayment period.

马斯克押注提前曝光？一个月前Reddit用户称特斯拉买入8亿美元比特币

Musk bet on early exposure? Amonth ago, a Reddit user claimed that Tesla bought $800 million in Bitcoin

该用户自称特斯拉软件研发人员，五周前发帖称，特斯拉72小时内以3.3142万美元的均价分批买入2.47万个比特币。此人相信，若媒体报道特斯拉买币后，币价会涨得更高，还说“以后再谢我”。

The user claimed to be a Tesla softwaredeveloper. Five weeks ago, he posted that Tesla bought 24,700 bitcoins inbatches at an average price of 33,142 US dollars within 72 hours. This personbelieved that if the media reported that Tesla bought the currency, the priceof the currency would rise even higher, and he said “Thank me againlater.”

十年十倍的超级机会？这是“牛市女皇”眼中比特币的未来

Ten times the super chance in tenyears? This is the future of Bitcoin in the eyes of the “Bull Queen”

ARK认为，比特币的基本面已经发生了巨大变化，机构是这波上涨的主要推手。有鉴于此，未来5到10年，比特币市值可能飙升至1万亿-5万亿美元，日均交易量将超过美股。
