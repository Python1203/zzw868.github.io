央行货币政策司司长孙国峰在《中国金融》撰文指出，货币政策要坚持稳健的总体取向，坚持“稳字当头”，保持战略定力，不左不右。

Sun Guofeng, Director of the MonetaryPolicy Department of the Central Bank, wrote in China Finance that monetarypolicy must adhere to a prudent overall orientation, adhere to a “stablecharacter as the head”, maintain strategic determination, and neither left norright.

证监会科技监管局局长：关于央行数字货币若干问题的思考
