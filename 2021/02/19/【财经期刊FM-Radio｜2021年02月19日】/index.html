要闻详情

净回笼2600亿元！央行等量续作2000亿MLF+200亿逆回购

A net return of 260 billion yuan!The central bank continues to make the same amount of 200 billion MLF + 20billion reverse repurchase

中国央行春节后现金逐步回笼。节后首个工作日，央行开展2000亿元1年期MLF操作以及200亿元逆回购操作，同日有2000亿元MLF叠加2800亿元逆回购到期。

After the Spring Festival, the People’sBank of China gradually returned cash. On the first working day after theholiday, the central bank carried out 200 billion yuan of 1-year MLF operationsand 20 billion yuan of reverse repurchase operations. On the same day, 200billion yuan of MLF superimposed on 280 billion yuan of reverse repurchaseexpired.

央行官媒再发文：当前已不应过度关注央行操作数量，否则可能对货币政策取向产生误解

The central bank’s official mediare-issued an article: At present, it is no longer necessary to pay too muchattention to the number of central bank operations, otherwise it maymisunderstand the monetary policy

当前重点关注的应当是央行公开市场操作利率、MLF利率等政策利率指标，以及市场基准利率在一段时间内的运行情况。

The current focus should be on the centralbank’s open market operating interest rate, MLF interest rate and other policyinterest rate indicators, as well as the operation of market benchmark interestrates over a period of time.

A股高开低走 抱团白马股集体下挫 各种“茅”大跌

A shares drove higher and lower,Baotuan Baima shares fell collectively, various “mao” fell sharply

与有色金属、影视的光鲜亮丽形成鲜明对比，节后基金热衷抱团的“各种茅”迎来当头一棒。

In sharp contrast with the glamour ofnon-ferrous metals and film and television, the “various mao” thatthe fund is keen to hold together after the festival has ushered in theheadlines.

重创特斯拉的“杀手”，开始重创茅台了？

The “killer” thatseverely inflicted Tesla, began to severely inflict Maotai?

像茅台这样备受国内外机构青睐的消费龙头股，其估值水平与短期业绩的波动正在脱钩，全球宏观利率、流动性、通胀等因素的影响最为重要。

Leading consumer stocks like Moutai, whichare favored by domestic and foreign institutions, are decoupling their valuationfrom short-term performance fluctuations. The impact of global macro interestrates, liquidity, inflation and other factors is the most important.

恒指一度跌超400点，科技指数跌3%，快手、美团跌超5%，阿里影业跌超9% TheHang Seng Index fell more than 400 points, the Technology Index fell 3%,Kuaishou and Meituan fell more than 5%, and Alibaba Pictures fell more than 9%

港股周四午后重挫，网易、阿里、小米跌超2%；此前活跃的餐饮股、SaaS概念股大幅下挫，阿里影业跌超9%，两日跌幅近15%。

Hong Kong stocks plummeted Thursdayafternoon, with Netease, Ali and Xiaomi falling more than 2%; previously activecatering stocks and SaaS concept stocks fell sharply, Ali Pictures fell more than9%, and nearly 15% in two days.

这个春节：消费开门红！新能源汽车订单翻10倍！电影票房高光，一票难求！

This Spring Festival: a goodstart for consumption! New energy vehicle orders doubled by 10 times! Movie boxoffice is high, hard to get a ticket!

据商务部监测，2月11日至17日，全国重点零售和餐饮企业实现销售额约8210亿元，比去年春节黄金周增长28.7%，比2019年春节黄金周增长4.9%。

According to monitoring by the Ministry ofCommerce, from February 11 to 17, the sales of key retail and cateringcompanies across the country reached approximately 821 billion yuan, anincrease of 28.7% over the Spring Festival Golden Week last year and anincrease of 4.9% over the 2019 Spring Festival Golden Week.

高瓴继续布局新能源 比亚迪确认其斥资逾15亿港元参与定增

Hillhouse continues to deploy newenergy, BYD confirms that it has spent more than HK$1.5 billion to participatein fixed increase

比亚迪品牌及公关事业部总经理李云飞向每日经济新闻确认了这一消息。值得关注的是，高瓴资本在此前刚刚清仓了造车新势力蔚来汽车、理想汽车。

Li Yunfei, general manager of BYD’s brandand public relations division, confirmed the news to the Daily Economic News.It is worth noting that Hillhouse Capital has just liquidated the newcar-making power Weilai Automobile and Ideal Automobile.

拥抱A股牛年首个“大肉签”吧，券商发30页报告高呼该股能涨340%
