Shanghai strictly controls and issues opinions to promote the steady andhealthy development of the real estate market

对夫妻离异3年内购买商品住房的，其拥有住房套数按离异前家庭总套数计算；个人将购买不足5年的住房对外销售的，全额征收增值税。

For couples who purchase commercial housingwithin 3 years of their divorce, the number of units owned by the couple iscalculated based on the total number of units of the family before the divorce;if an individual purchases less than 5 years of housing for external sales, thefull value-added tax will be levied.

The Growth Enterprise Market Index rose over 3%, breaking through 3300points! Group stocks such as liquor and automobiles continue to rise sharply

稀土永磁现涨停潮，引领资源股全线走强。诊断概念、光伏、军工、券商等也大幅走高。

Rare earth permanent magnets are nowtrading at a daily limit, leading resource stocks to strengthen across theboard. Diagnostic concepts, photovoltaics, military industry, and securitiescompanies also rose sharply.

港股午后深V行情恒指3万点得而复失科技医疗普遍回调

Hong Kong stocks afternoon Shenzhen V market, the Hang Seng Index gainedand lost 30,000 points

早盘恒指微幅低开0.04%后上冲，一度站上3万点，创2019年5月来新高，随后震荡回落。科技股、医疗保健板块普遍回调。中芯国际下跌5.1%，领跌恒生科技指数成份股。

In early trading, the Hang Seng Indexopened slightly lower by 0.04% and then rushed up. It once stood above 30,000points, hitting a new high since May 2019, and then fell back in shock.Technology stocks and healthcare sectors generally recovered. SMIC fell 5.1%,leading the decline in constituent stocks of the Hang Seng Technology Index.

视频成了互联网最新风口？微信之后，网易也宣布进军了！

Video has become the latest trend of the Internet? After WeChat, NetEasealso announced its march!

在1月21日网易举办的视频创作者线上大会上，网易方面宣布，将启动“NEXT创作家计划”，以“十亿现金、百亿流量、千亿曝光”扶持创作者。微信此前也宣布扶持视频内容的战略；对于内容载体进化，巨头们判断惊人一致：视频化表达，可能是下一个十年内容领域的主题。

At the online video creators conferenceheld by NetEase on January 21, NetEase announced that it would launch the”NEXT Creators Program” to support creators with “billions ofcash, tens of billions of traffic, and 100 billions of exposure.” WeChatalso announced a strategy to support video content before; regarding theevolution of content carriers, the giants’ judgments are surprisinglyconsistent: video expression may be the theme of the content field in the nextdecade.

市值超6000亿巨头大举配售300亿港元将加大对新能源车布局

Giants with a market value of more than 600 billion will sell 30 billionHong Kong dollars to increase their new energy vehicle layout

比亚迪最新配售动作也与管理层长期观点一致；公司创始人王传福此前预测，未来5年行业年复合增长率或达37%，电动车全面替代燃油车的时机已成熟。

BYD’s latest placement moves are alsoconsistent with the management’s long-term view; the company’s founder WangChuanfu previously predicted that the industry’s compound annual growth ratecould reach 37% in the next five years, and the time is ripe for electricvehicles to fully replace fuel vehicles.
