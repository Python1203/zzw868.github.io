Whether this situation willintensify in the future depends on whether the monetary policy is to be shiftedappropriately this year. If it does not, these problems will certainlycontinue, which will lead to greater economic and financial risks in the mediumand long term.

The Spring Festival holiday is approachingand funding is tight. Analysts predict that the Spring Festival this year willbe similar to previous years, with a liquidity gap of about 1 trillion yuan,and funding will be tight first and then loose.

The tightness of bank money is only ashort-term disturbance, but what needs special reminder is that the time whenmoney liquidity is the most loose has long passed.

如何理解上午利率的快速上行？

How to understand the rapid riseof interest rates in the morning?

周二早盘，国债收益率曲线平坦化上行。3-5年活跃券收益率多数上行5bp，10年活跃券利率则上行3bp左右。隔夜回购利率则创逾15个月新高。屈庆认为在疫情形势尚不明朗的情况下，央行不存在过度收紧货币政策的可能性；考虑春节流动性缺口，央行后续必有更多资金进行对冲，回购利率大概率会触顶回落。

In early trading on Tuesday, the Treasurybond yield curve flattened upward. Most of the 3-5-year active bond yields wentup by 5bp, and the 10-year active bond yields went up by about 3bp. Theovernight repurchase rate hit a new high in more than 15 months. Qu Qingbelieves that when the epidemic situation is still unclear, the central bankdoes not have the possibility of excessively tightening monetary policy;considering the liquidity gap during the Spring Festival, the central bank willhave more funds to hedge in the future, and the repurchase interest rate willmost likely peak and fall .

点评：据了解，钴原料产地刚果（金）和物流中转地南非疫情进一步加重。中金公司[601995.SS、03908.HKEX]表示，南非疫情反复，引发市场对于钴供给的担忧，下游厂商加大了补库存力度。在下游的旺盛需求和较高的原料成本支撑下，钴价短期内具备上行基础。寒锐钴业、洛阳钼业[603993.SS、03993.HKEX]等主营钴相关产品。
