中国央行会议要求，强化支付领域监管，个人征信业务必须持牌经营，严禁金融产品过度营销，诱导过度负债，严肃查处侵害金融消费者合法权益的违法违规行为。确保金融创新在审慎监管前提下发展。

Ensure that financialinnovation develops under the premise of prudential supervision.

The five departments jointlyissued a document: Cancel unreasonable charges for water, electricity, gas, andheating! Monopoly is strictly prohibited!

加快推进竞争性环节的市场化。建立健全以“准许成本+合理收益”为核心的定价机制，合理制定并动态调整供水、供电、供气、供暖价格。

Accelerate the marketization of competitivelinks. Establish and improve a pricing mechanism centered on “permittedcosts + reasonable benefits”, and reasonably formulate and dynamicallyadjust prices for water, power, gas, and heating.

住建部：时刻绷紧房地产市场调控这根弦，加快研究编制“十四五”住房发展规划

Ministry of Housing and Urban-RuralDevelopment: Always tighten the string of real estate market regulation andspeed up the study and preparation of the “14th Five-Year” housingdevelopment plan

加强对重点城市的指导，实施精准调控。支持人口净流入的大城市发展共有产权住房，供应范围逐步扩大到常住人口。在人口净流入的大城市重点发展政策性租赁住房。

Strengthen guidance to key cities andimplement precise control. Support the development of shared property housingin big cities with a net population inflow, and gradually expand the scope ofsupply to the permanent population. Focus on the development of policy-basedrental housing in large cities with net population inflows.

中国12月财新服务业PMI回落至56.3 但仍位于过去十年来高位

China’s Caixin service industryPMI fell to 56.3 in December, but it is still at the highest level in the pastdecade

中国服务业活动进一步扩张，增速虽未及此前两月，但仍是过去十年来最高记录之一，说明服务业从2020年初爆发的新冠疫情中复苏。

China’s service industry activities haveexpanded further. Although the growth rate is not as fast as the previous twomonths, it is still one of the highest records in the past decade, indicatingthat the service industry has recovered from the new crown epidemic that brokeout in early 2020.

中字头股票集体爆发 沪深300指数突破2015年高点

Chinese stocks explodedcollectively, Shanghai and Shenzhen 300 Index broke 2015 high

周三A股沪指早盘冲高回落，沪深300指数也随之来到近13年新高。Wind数据显示，代表行业龙头走势的沪深300指数盘中突破2015年高点，最高触及5433.47点，为2008年1月以来首次。分析称，未来不管十年之后，A股公司从四千到六千家，最有价值还是前20%，还是核心资产。

On Wednesday, the A-share Shanghai Indexrose and fell in early trading, and the Shanghai and Shenzhen 300 Index alsoreached a new high in the past 13 years. Wind data shows that the Shanghai andShenzhen 300 Index, which represents the industry’s leading trend, brokethrough the high point in 2015 during the intraday session, reaching a maximumof 5433.47 points, the first time since January 2008. According to theanalysis, in the next ten years, the number of A-share companies from 4,000 to6,000 will be the top 20% or core assets.
