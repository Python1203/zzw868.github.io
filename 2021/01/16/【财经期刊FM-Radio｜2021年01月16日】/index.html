“Seeking Truth”published an important article by General Secretary Xi Jinping “Correctlyunderstand and grasp the major issues of medium and long-term economic andsocial development”

文章指出，要以科技创新催生新发展动能。大力提升自主创新能力，尽快突破关键核心技术。

The article pointed out that it isnecessary to use technological innovation to generate new development momentum.Vigorously improve the ability of independent innovation and make breakthroughsin key core technologies as soon as possible.

央行：M2、社融增速不宜再设置数字目标，目前存款准备金率水平不高

Central Bank: M2. It is no longerappropriate to set a digital target for the growth rate of social financing,and the current deposit reserve ratio is not high

央行孙国峰认为，M2、社融增速不宜再设置数字目标，货币政策应在多重目标中寻求动态平衡。

Sun Guofeng, the central bank, believesthat M2, the growth rate of social finance should no longer set digitaltargets, and monetary policy should seek a dynamic balance among multipletargets.

央行：坚持稳字当头不急转弯，蚂蚁集团已成立整改工作组

The Central Bank: Adhere tosteady words and do not make a sharp turn, Ant Group has established arectification work group

去年房地产贷款增速是8年来首次低于各项贷款增速。孙国峰认为，不论是横向比较还是纵向比较，目前我国的存款准备金率水平不高。

The growth rate of real estate loans lastyear was lower than the growth rate of various loans for the first time ineight years. Sun Guofeng believes that the current level of my country’sdeposit reserve ratio is not high whether it is horizontal or verticalcomparison.

新年第一碗“麻辣粉”！中国央行缩量续作5000亿元MLF

The first bowl of”Spicy Noodles” in the New Year! China’s central bank shrinks tocontinue making 500 billion MLF

利率维持在2.95%不变。周五有3000亿元MLF到期，1月25日还有2405亿元TMLF到期。

The interest rate remained unchanged at2.95%. On Friday, 300 billion yuan of MLF expires, and 240.5 billion yuan ofTMLF expires on January 25.

央行金融研究所：“本币优先、本币主导”

Central BankInstitute of Finance: “Local currency first, local currency dominant”

本币主导、本币优先，在中国企业对外产业转移过程中优先使用人民币，为供应链调整、产业结构升级和价值链跃迁提供更好的保障、支持和服务。

The local currency is dominant and thelocal currency is given priority. The renminbi is given priority in the processof Chinese enterprises’ foreign industrial transfer, to provide betterguarantee, support and services for supply chain adjustment, industrialstructure upgrading and value chain transition.

A股指数全线翻红！白酒、光伏、军工等抱团股跌幅显著收窄

A-share index became popularacross the board! Group stocks such as liquor, photovoltaics, and militaryindustries have significantly narrowed their declines

午后三大指数探底回升，创业板指一度跌超2%又翻红，沪指微涨0.2%。“抱团股”出现明显回调。

In the afternoon, the three major indexesbottomed out and rebounded. The ChiNext stock index fell more than 2% andturned red, and the Shanghai stock index rose 0.2%. “Baotuan stocks”showed a significant correction.

招行股价再创新高 惊喜不只是盈利修复

China Merchants Bank’s shareprice hits new highs, surprises are not just profit restoration

开年以来，银行板块先抑后扬，虽然新年前两个交易日受房贷集中度管理制度影响有所回调，但此后迅速反弹，近7个交易日内板块整体涨幅达6.72%。

Since the beginning of the year, thebanking sector has declined first and then increased. Although the housing loanconcentration management system has been adjusted in the first two trading daysof the new year, it has rebounded rapidly since then. The overall increase inthe sector reached 6.72% in the past seven trading days.

四部委：完善金融机构债委会制度，支持金融机构联合打击逃废金融债务

Four ministries: improve the debtcommittee system of financial institutions and support financial institutionsto jointly combat evasion of financial debt

明确对债务规模较大、存在困难的非金融债务企业，3家以上持有债权的银行保险机构和证券期货基金经营机构等可以发起成立债委会。

It is clear that non-financial debtcompanies with large debts and difficulties, three or more bancassuranceinstitutions and securities and futures fund management institutions that holdcreditor rights can initiate the establishment of debt committees.

“做好农村地区疫情防控工作，抓好农业生产和农产品供给”！两部委发重磅通知 “Do a good jobin the prevention and control of the epidemic in rural areas, and do a good jobin agricultural production and supply of agricultural products”! Twoministries issued heavy notices

要切实抓好疫情中高风险地区农产品供应，严禁未经批准擅自设卡拦截、随意断路封路阻断交通，不得随意以防疫为借口拦截农资运输车辆。

It is necessary to earnestly grasp thesupply of agricultural products in high-risk areas of the epidemic. It isstrictly forbidden to set up cards to intercept without approval, cut off roadsat will and block roads to block traffic, and do not use epidemic prevention asan excuse to intercept agricultural transportation vehicles.

基础设施领域REITs试点来了！

The pilot of REITs in the fieldof infrastructure is here!
