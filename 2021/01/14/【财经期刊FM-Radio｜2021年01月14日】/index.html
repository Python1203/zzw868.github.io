The three major A-share indexes fluctuated and closed down

A total of 1083 stocks in the two citiesrose, 3,065 stocks fell, and some Baima stocks began to loosen up. Arowana fellmore than 7%, China CDF fell 6%, and CATL fell more than 4%.

央行周三逆回购仅20亿，创十年最低！有何深意？

The central bank’s reverse repurchase was only 2 billion on Wednesday,the lowest in ten years! What’s the meaning?

中国央行周三公开市场开展逆回购操作以补充流动性，然而，区区20亿元的规模，为近十年来最低水平。有评论戏称：“投的不是钱，而是态度。”

The central bank of China launched areverse repurchase operation on the open market on Wednesday to supplementliquidity. However, the scale of 2 billion yuan is the lowest level in the pastdecade. Some commentators joked: “It’s not money, but attitude.”

央行论文：保持M2和社融增速与名义GDP增速“基本匹配”不意味着“完全相等” Central Bank Paper:Keeping M2 and social financial growth “basically matched” withnominal GDP growth does not mean “completely equal”

央行货币政策司司长孙国峰称，M2和社融增速可以根据需要略高或略低于名义经济增速。操作上是按年度做到基本匹配，而不是每个季度甚至每个月都要匹配。

According to Sun Guofeng, Director of theMonetary Policy Department of the Central Bank, the growth rate of M2 andsocial financing can be slightly higher or slightly lower than the nominaleconomic growth rate as needed. The operation is to do basic matching on an annualbasis, not every quarter or even every month.

监管新规剑指消费金融公司！银保监会发布重磅监管评级新规

New regulatory regulations point to consumer finance companies! ChinaBanking and Insurance Regulatory Commission issued new regulations for heavyregulatory ratings

消费金融公司监管评级要素包括五方面：公司治理与内控（28%），资本管理（12%），风险管理（35%），专业服务质量（15%），信息科技管理（10%）。

Consumer finance companies’ regulatory ratingelements include five aspects: corporate governance and internal control (28%),capital management (12%), risk management (35%), professional service quality(15%), and information technology management (10%).

拼多多买菜进击一线城市

Pinduoduo to enter first-tier cities

1月12日是多多买菜在上海开团的第一天，在上海部分地区，一个居民社区1公里内已经开出了11家自提点。

January 12th was the first day ofDuoduomai’s group in Shanghai. In some areas of Shanghai, 11 self-pickup spotshave been opened within 1 km of a residential community.

中汽协：12月新能源乘用车销售同比大增近80%，连续6个月产销刷新记录

China Automobile Association: In December, sales of new energy passengervehicles increased by nearly 80% year-on-year, setting new records inproduction and sales for 6 consecutive months

中汽协预计，新能源汽车2021年销量将同比增长40%。

The China Automobile Association predictsthat the sales of new energy vehicles in 2021 will increase by 40%year-on-year.

B站确定已在港提交上市申请计划3月上市

Station B confirmed that it has submitted a listing application in HongKong and plans to go public in March

B站已经向港交所正式提交了上市申请，计划3月在港上市，其此次上市保荐团队为摩根士丹利、摩根大通、高盛和瑞银。

Station B has officially submitted alisting application to the Hong Kong Stock Exchange and plans to list in HongKong in March. Its sponsoring team for this listing is Morgan Stanley, JPMorgan Chase, Goldman Sachs and UBS.

上汽、阿里联手打造的智己汽车发布两款纯电新车！最快今年底上市

The Zhiji Automobile jointly built by SAIC and Ali released two new pureelectric cars! Listed as soon as this year

智能纯电轿车将在今年4月的上海车展期间接受全球预订，2021年底上市，2022年交付，智能纯电SUV将于2022年正式上市。周三美股午盘，阿里巴巴ADR涨2.9%，领跑其他中概造车新势力。

Smart pure electric sedans will acceptglobal pre-orders during the Shanghai Auto Show in April this year. They willbe launched at the end of 2021 and delivered in 2022. Smart pure electric SUVswill be officially launched in 2022. At midday in the US stock market onWednesday, Alibaba’s ADR rose 2.9%, leading other new forces in China to buildcars.

摩根士丹利：中概股估值有风险，A股没有

Morgan Stanley: China concept stock valuation is risky, A shares do not

大摩认为，在海外上市的中概股由于更多地暴露在外部风险之中，其估值已经风险较大，但A股由于面对外部不确定性的韧性较强，加上资本市场改革和中国经济强劲基本面的支撑，反而风险较小。

Morgan Stanley believes that China conceptstocks listed overseas are more exposed to external risks, and their valuationsare already relatively risky, but A-shares are more resilient in the face ofexternal uncertainties and capital market reforms. And the support of thestrong fundamentals of the Chinese economy, on the contrary, the risk isrelatively small.

神农投资陈宇：这一轮牛市时长史无前例，遇到几何级增长的大风口不能采用通用估值办法

Shennong Investment Chen Yu: The duration of this round of bull marketis unprecedented, and general valuation methods cannot be used whenencountering large vents of geometric growth.

市场已经发生了最大的变化——投资人目前面对着历史上最大的牛市，这是基于最底层的变化，注册制改革引发的牛市。投资策略必须改变，要all in这个时代top 3的风口及其王者。

The market has undergone the biggestchanges-investors are currently facing the largest bull market in history,which is based on the changes at the bottom level and the bull market triggeredby the reform of the registration system. The investment strategy must bechanged, to be all in the top 3 of this era and its king.

中国债市的特殊现象：尾盘利率加速下行！原因何在？

A special phenomenon in China’s bond market: interest rates at the endof the day have accelerated! What is the reason?

近期，利率经常在全天大部分交易时间内保持平稳，但下午3点以后出现一波显著下行。不止近期如此，过去几年的牛熊周期中也经常出现3点以后快速上行或下行的情况。

Recently, interest rates have oftenremained stable during most of the trading hours of the day, but there has beena significant decline after 3 pm. This is not only the case in the recent past,but also in the bull-bear cycle of the past few years, there have been rapidupward or downward movements after 3 o’clock.
