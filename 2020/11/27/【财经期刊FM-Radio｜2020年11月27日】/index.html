要闻详情

国常会：确定促进征信业发展提质的措施，依法严惩征信评级弄虚作假等行为

National StandingCommittee: Determine measures to promote the development and quality of creditreporting industry, and severely punish fraudulent credit reporting and otheracts in accordance with the law

据中国政府网，国务院总理李克强11月25日主持召开国务院常务会议，会议确定规范和完善失信约束制度、健全社会信用体系的措施，还确定了促进征信业发展提质的措施，要求推进金融、政务、公用事业等相关征信信息跨领域、跨地域依法共享，完善征信配套法规制度，强化问责机制，对征信评级弄虚作假等行为要依法严惩。
According to China Government Network,Premier Li Keqiang hosted an executive meeting of the State Council on November25. The meeting determined measures to standardize and improve the untrustworthyrestraint system and improve the social credit system. It also determinedmeasures to promote the development and quality of the credit investigationindustry, and required the promotion of finance , Government affairs, publicutilities and other related credit information are shared across domains andregions in accordance with the law, improve credit reporting supporting lawsand systems, strengthen accountability mechanisms, and severely punishfraudulent credit ratings.

中国央行三季度货币执行报告：既不让市场缺钱，又不让市场的钱溢出来

The third quartermonetary execution report of the People’s Bank of China: neither let the marketbe short of money nor let the market’s money overflow

央行在报告中称，下一阶段，将完善货币供应调控机制，根据宏观形势和市场需要，科学把握货币政策操作的力度、节奏和重点，既保持流动性合理充裕，不让市场缺钱，又坚决不搞“大水漫灌”，不让市场的钱溢出来。此外，央行新增专门段落描述金融风险预防、预警、处置、问责制度体系。
The central bank stated in the report thatin the next stage, it will improve the money supply control mechanism, andscientifically grasp the intensity, rhythm and focus of monetary policyoperations in accordance with the macro situation and market needs, so as tomaintain reasonable and sufficient liquidity and prevent the market from beingshort of money. Resolutely do not engage in “sweep flooding” andprevent the market from overflowing. In addition, the central bank has added aspecial paragraph describing the system of financial risk prevention, earlywarning, disposal, and accountability.

中国央行：正确理解央行资产负债表与货币供应的关系

The Central Bank ofChina: Correctly understand the relationship between the central bank’s balancesheet and money supply

央行货币政策执行报告专栏文章称，传统货币乘数观点反映了对货币创造的机械式观察，未充分认识到商业银行才是货币创造的主体，已不适用于对货币的现实分析。观察货币政策的松紧，关键还是看货币信贷是否合理增长。
The central bank’s monetary policyimplementation report column said that the traditional currency multiplier viewreflects a mechanical observation of currency creation, and it has not fullyrealized that commercial banks are the main body of currency creation, and itis no longer suitable for realistic analysis of currency. Observing thetightness of monetary policy, the key is to see whether money and credit growreasonably

广电总局：支持广电机构控股或参股互联网企业、科技企业，开展跨所有制并购重组

The StateAdministration of Radio, Film and Television: Supporting radio and televisioninstitutions to hold or participate in Internet companies, technologycompanies, and carry out cross-ownership mergers

广电总局通知还称，加快把分散的、优质的资源要素向互联网主阵地聚合、向移动端倾斜；运用主流价值导向驾驭“算法”。
The SARFT notice also stated that it willaccelerate the integration of dispersed and high-quality resource elements tothe main Internet position and tilt toward the mobile terminal; use mainstreamvalue orientation to control “algorithms.”

任正非送别荣耀：不要藕断丝连要做华为最强的竞争对手
