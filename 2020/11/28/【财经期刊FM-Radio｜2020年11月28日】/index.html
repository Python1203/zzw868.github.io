中国10月工业企业利润同比大增28.2% 前10月增速年内首次转正

The profit ofChina’s industrial enterprises in October increased by 28.2% year-on-year, andthe growth rate in the first 10 months turned positive for the first time thisyear

国家统计局工业司高级统计师朱虹认为，10月工业企业利润增速大幅增长，主要是去年同期基数较低、本月投资收益增加等因素共同作用的结果。剔除短期非经营因素影响后，10月份利润增速与上月大体相当。
Zhu Hong, a senior statistician at theDepartment of Industry of the National Bureau of Statistics, believes that thesharp increase in the profit growth of industrial enterprises in October wasmainly due to the combination of factors such as a low base in the same periodlast year and an increase in investment income this month. After excluding theimpact of short-term non-operating factors, profit growth in October wasroughly the same as the previous month.

周小川：资产价格不纳入通胀考虑已不行了，怎么纳入还需研究

Zhou Xiaochuan: It’sno longer enough if asset prices are not included in inflation considerations.How to include them still needs to be studied

周小川认为，传统的通胀度量现在面临包括较少包含资产价格会带来失真、以什么收入作为计算通胀的支出篮子等方面的不足和挑战。过去投资品价格和资产价格可另行考虑，现在再这样做恐怕已经不行了。
Zhou Xiaochuan believes that traditionalinflation measures are now faced with deficiencies and challenges includingless including asset prices that will cause distortion, and what income is usedas the expenditure basket for calculating inflation. In the past, investmentproduct prices and asset prices could be considered separately, but it may nolonger be possible to do so now.

Ifthe shares of the Sci-tech Innovation Board are SSE 180, SSE 380 Indexconstituent stocks or A+H shares company’s A shares, the stock will betransferred to the scope of Shanghai Stock Connect according to the existingregulations of Shanghai-Hong Kong Stock Connect, and the corresponding H shareswill be The existing regulations of Shenzhen-Hong Kong Stock Connect aretransferred to the scope of Southbound Stock Connect stocks.

The Hong Kong StockExchange plans to raise the threshold for listing: the net profit of the firsttwo fiscal years is not less than 75 million

证监会对永煤控股及相关审计机构涉嫌债券违法行为立案调查
