Proposal of the ShanghaiMunicipal Committee of the Communist Party of China on the formulation of theFourteenth Five-Year Plan for the National Economic and Social Development ofShanghai and the Long-term Goals for 2035

“十四五”时期是我国全面建成小康社会、实现第一个百年奋斗目标之后，乘势而上开启全面建设社会主义现代化国家新征程、向第二个百年奋斗目标进军的第一个五年，也是上海在新的起点上全面深化“五个中心”建设、加快建设具有世界影响力的社会主义现代化国际大都市的关键五年。中国共产党上海市第十一届委员会第十次全体会议深入学习贯彻中国共产党第十九届中央委员会第五次全体会议精神，深入贯彻落实习近平总书记考察上海重要讲话精神，深入分析国际国内形势和事关上海长远发展的重大问题，就制定国民经济和社会发展“十四五”规划和二〇三五年远景目标提出建议。

The “14th Five-Year Plan” periodis the first five years after my country has built a moderately prosperoussociety in an all-round way and achieved its first centenary goal, and thentook advantage of the momentum to embark on a new journey of building asocialist modern country in an all-round way and march towards the secondcentenary goal. It is also the key five years for Shanghai to comprehensivelydeepen the construction of the “five centers” from a new startingpoint and accelerate the construction of a socialist modern internationalmetropolis with world influence. The tenth plenary meeting of the 11th ShanghaiCommittee of the Communist Party of China thoroughly studied and implementedthe spirit of the fifth plenary meeting of the 19th Central Committee of theCommunist Party of China, thoroughly implemented the spirit of GeneralSecretary Jin Ping’s important speech in Shanghai, and deeply analyzed theinternational and domestic situations. For major issues related to Shanghai’slong-term development, we put forward suggestions on the formulation of the”14th Five-Year Plan” for national economic and social developmentand long-term goals for 2035.

中国11月新增社融环比大增50%，居民企业中长期贷款大增，M2-M1继续收窄

China’s new social financingincreased by 50% month-on-month in November, medium and long-term loans toresidential enterprises increased significantly, and M2-M1 continued to narrow

12月9日，中国央行公布11月金融数据，其中社融数据、新增人民币贷款数据止跌反弹，狭义货币供应量M1增速攀升至10%，创下34个月新高。

On December 9, the People’s Bank of Chinaannounced financial data for November. Among them, social financial data andnew renminbi loan data stopped falling and rebounded. The growth rate of narrowmoney supply M1 climbed to 10%, a 34-month high.
