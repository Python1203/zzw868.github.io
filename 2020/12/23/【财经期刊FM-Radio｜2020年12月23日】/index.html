醉酒当歌！多只个股涨停，茅台五粮液洋河泸州老窖齐创历史新高！

Drunk as a song! Many stocks havetheir daily limit, Maotai Wuliangye Yanghe Luzhou Laojiao set a new record!

Wuliangye rose even more sharply during the year, up to 120%,with a market value of more than 1.11 trillion yuan. Huangtai Liquor, Jiugui Liquor,ST Willing and other stocks have increased by more than 200% during the year.

国常会：延续普惠小微企业贷款延期还本付息政策和信用贷款支持计划

The State Council: Continuationof the deferred principal and interest payment policy and credit loan supportplan for inclusive small and micro enterprise loans

会议确定，明年一季度要继续落实好原定的普惠小微企业贷款延期还本付息政策，在此基础上适当延长政策期限，做到按市场化原则应延尽延。

The meeting determined that in the firstquarter of next year, we will continue to implement the original policy ofdeferring principal and interest payments for inclusive small and microenterprise loans. On this basis, the policy period should be appropriately extendedto ensure that the extension should be extended in accordance with the marketprinciple.

证监会：促进居民储蓄向投资转化，大力发展权益类公募基金

China Securities RegulatoryCommission: Promote the conversion of household savings to investment, andvigorously develop equity public funds

证监会表示，下一步着力加强资本市场投资端建设，增强财富管理功能，促进居民储蓄向投资转化，助力扩大内需。

The China Securities Regulatory Commissionstated that the next step is to strengthen the construction of the investmentside of the capital market, enhance the wealth management function, promote theconversion of household savings to investment, and help expand domestic demand.

适当牺牲大量进口短期优势？适时动态监管资金流向流量？央行参事室主任提了建议

Appropriately sacrifice theshort-term advantages of large imports? Timely and dynamically monitoring theflow of funds? The director of the Central Bank Counselor’s Office madesuggestions

央行参事室主任纪敏还建议，引导鼓励资管产品逐步增加权益投资、信用债券投资比重，加大对新能源等产业的金融支持，同时，金融监管要“管住人、看住钱、扎牢制度防火墙”。中国央行官方微信号发表央行参事室主任纪敏的政策研究论文：《高质量“双循环”及金融供给侧结构性改革》。

Ji Min, Director of the Counselor’s Officeof the Central Bank, also suggested that asset management products should beencouraged to gradually increase the proportion of equity investment and creditbond investment, and to increase financial support for industries such as newenergy. At the same time, financial supervision should “control people, keepmoney, Secure the system firewall”. The official WeChat account of thePeople’s Bank of China published a policy research paper by Ji Min, Director ofthe Counselor Office of the People’s Bank of China: “High-quality”Double Cycles” and Financial Supply-Side Structural Reform.”

1年新增1.2亿千瓦，新能源2021年目标基本敲定

120 million kilowatts will beadded in a year, and the new energy target for 2021 is basically finalized

12月22日，中国能源政策研究年会2020暨“中国电力圆桌”四季度会议在京召开。有代表在会上表示，目前国家能源局已经提出了“2021年我国风电、太阳能发电合计新增1.2亿千瓦”的目标。该消息也得到了国家能源局相关人士的证实。这标志着我国新能源行业年度新增装机规划将首次突破1亿千瓦。

On December 22, the China Energy PolicyResearch Annual Conference 2020 and the fourth quarter meeting of the”China Electric Power Roundtable” were held in Beijing. Somerepresentatives said at the meeting that the National Energy Administration hasnow put forward the goal of “a total of 120 million kilowatts of windpower and solar power in my country in 2021”. The news has also beenconfirmed by relevant persons from the National Energy Administration. Thismarks the first time that my country’s new energy industry’s annual newinstalled capacity plan will exceed 100 million kilowatts.

四川银保监局：四川信托被实施管控，对4家股东采取监管强制措施

Sichuan Banking and InsuranceRegulatory Bureau: Sichuan Trust is under control, and mandatory regulatorymeasures are taken against 4 shareholders

四川银保监局将联合地方政府派出工作组，加强对四川信托的管控，督促其尽快改组董事会，防止风险敞口扩大。

The Sichuan Banking and InsuranceRegulatory Bureau will send a working group in conjunction with the localgovernment to strengthen the management and control of Sichuan Trust and urgeit to reorganize the board of directors as soon as possible to prevent theexpansion of risk exposure.

恒生指数或迎大调整：成份股数量扩容，降低大型成份股权重

The Hang Seng Index may welcomemajor adjustments: the number of constituent stocks will be expanded and theweight of large constituent stocks will be reduced

恒指公司建议将成份股数量从现在的50只左右扩充至65至80只；将“非同股不同权/第二上市公司成份股为10%比重上限”和“同股不同权/第二上市公司成份股为5%比重上限”统一为“所有成份股为8%比重上限”。

The Hang Seng Index Company proposes toexpand the number of constituent stocks from the current 50 to 65 to 80; setthe “non-same share with different rights/second listed company constituentstocks to the 10% upper limit” and “same share with different rights/ second “The constituent stocks of listed companies have an upper limitof 5%” unified as “all constituent stocks have an upper limit of8%.”

社区团购“九不得”新规出台

Community group buying “ninemust not” new regulations introduced

市场监管总局联合商务部22日召开规范社区团购秩序行政指导会，阿里、腾讯、京东、美团、拼多多、滴滴6家互联网平台企业参加。

The State Administration of MarketSupervision and the Ministry of Commerce held an administrative guidancemeeting on regulating the order of community group purchases on the 22nd. SixInternet platform companies including Ali, Tencent, JD.com, Meituan, Pinduoduo,and Didi participated.
