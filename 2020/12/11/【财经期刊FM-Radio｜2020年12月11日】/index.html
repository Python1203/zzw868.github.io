Judging from thecurrent development situation, the demand for photovoltaic power generation inthe “14th Five-Year Plan” will be much higher than that in the”Thirteenth Five-Year Plan”. Therefore, it is necessary to furthermobilize the enthusiasm of photovoltaic power generation enterprises and otherentities.

The final red linefor bank wealth management “hard bone” asset clean-up is delineated,and old products will be relaxed until 2025 at the latest.

If thetransition period of the new asset management regulations at the end of 2021has not been resolved, one line and one policy can be adopted. Relax late until2025.

泡泡玛特暗盘涨超100%，市值突破千亿港元

Honor CEO Zhao Ming clearlystated at the employee communication meeting that Honor’s goal is to become thenumber one in the domestic mobile phone market.
