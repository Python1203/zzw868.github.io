In addition,the People’s Bank of China will carry out the Mid-term Lending Facility (MLF)operation on December 15 (including the one-time renewal of the two MLF due onDecember 7 and 16), and the specific operation amount will be determinedaccording to market demand and other conditions .

Guo Shuqing pointed out that real estate isdeeply connected with the financial industry. At present, my country’s realestate-related loans account for 39% of banking loans, and a large amount ofbonds, equity, trusts and other funds enter the real estate industry. It can besaid that real estate is the biggest “gray rhino” in terms offinancial risks in my country at this stage.
