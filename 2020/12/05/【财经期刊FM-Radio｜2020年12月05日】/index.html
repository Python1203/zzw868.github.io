市场概述

跨党派刺激案得到更多美国议员支持，国会领袖有意将刺激案与避免本月政府关门的计划捆绑，加之非农就业人数远逊预期可能激发推出新刺激的紧迫性，两党在年底前达成刺激协议的希望升温，美股走高，三大股指齐创新高，全周再度累计上涨。得益于原油、铁矿石和铜上行的能源股支持，泛欧股指继续上涨，连涨五周，英股全周表现最佳。

美元指数又创逾两年新低，连跌三周；离岸人民币创两年多新高，英镑创逾两年新高后转跌。原油涨超1%创九个月新高，伦铜全周第三日创逾七年新高，均连涨五周。黄金止步三连涨跌落近两周高位，但金银全周均终结三周累跌。10年期美债收益率创8个月新高。

要闻详情

银保监会课题组发布重磅报告 影子银行首个官方定义来了！

The China Banking and InsuranceRegulatory Commission’s research team released a heavy report The firstofficial definition of shadow banking is here!

本报告详细描述了影子银行的起源和发展演变，中国影子银行的判别标准、范围和规模，以及影子银行三年治理的背景、过程和成效等，并提出相应的监管建议。
This report describes in detail the origin,development and evolution of shadow banking, the criteria, scope, and scale ofshadow banking in China, as well as the background, process, and effectivenessof shadow banking for three years, and puts forward corresponding regulatoryrecommendations.

中国证监会谈美国批准《外国公司问责法案》：坚决反对将证券监管政治化的做法

China Securities RegulatoryCommission talks about the approval of the “Foreign Company AccountabilityAct” in the United States: Resolutely oppose the practice of politicizingsecurities supervision

美国对外国发行人提出的额外披露要求具有明显的歧视性。美国监管机构暂时不能检查为在美上市中国公司提供审计服务的中国会计师事务所，是跨境监管合作领域的问题，应当通过加强双边监管合作加以解决。
The additional disclosure requirements ofthe United States against foreign issuers are obviously discriminatory. TheU.S. regulatory agencies are temporarily unable to inspect Chinese accountingfirms that provide audit services to Chinese companies listed in the U.S. Thisis a problem in the field of cross-border regulatory cooperation and should beresolved by strengthening bilateral regulatory cooperation.

香港金管局：正与中国人民银行数字货币研究所，研究使用数字人民币进行跨境支付技术测试。

Hong Kong MonetaryAuthority: It is working with the Digital Currency Research Institute of thePeople’s Bank of China to study the use of digital RMB for cross-border paymenttechnology testing.

香港金融管理局总裁余伟文撰文称，当前，金管局正与中国人民银行数字货币研究所研究使用数字人民币进行跨境支付的技术测试，并作相应的技术准备。
Yu Weiwen, president of the Hong KongMonetary Authority, wrote an article that currently, the Monetary Authority isworking with the Digital Currency Research Institute of the People’s Bank ofChina to study the use of digital renminbi for cross-border payment technologytesting and make corresponding technical preparations.

折价10.2%？媒体称理想汽车增发价29美元，拟募资13.6亿美元

10.2% discount? The media saidthat the ideal car’s additional issuance price was US$29, and it plans to raiseUS$1.36 billion

彭博称，理想汽车把增发4700万股ADS的价格定在29美元，较周四收盘折价10.2％。该股自11月24日以来大幅回调，迄今累计跌超三成。周五，理想汽车跌超5%。
According to Bloomberg, Ideal Auto set theprice of additional 47 million ADS shares at $29, a 10.2% discount toThursday’s closing price. The stock has corrected sharply since November 24 andhas fallen by more than 30% so far. On Friday, Ideal Car fell more than 5%.

大幅不及预期！美国11月非农就业人口新增24.5万人，预期47.5万人

Much less than expected! USnon-agricultural employment population increased by 245,000 in November, and isexpected to be 475,000

美国11月非农业就业人口新增24.5万人，大幅低于市场预期的47.5万人，前值63.8万。11月，美国失业率为6.7%，略好于市场预期的6.8%，和前值6.9%相比亦有所收窄。11月也是自4月美国失业率达到14.7%之后连续第七个月下降。
The non-agricultural employment populationof the United States increased by 245,000 in November, which was significantlylower than market expectations of 475,000 and the previous value was 638,000.In November, the US unemployment rate was 6.7%, slightly better than marketexpectations of 6.8%, and also narrowed from the previous value of 6.9%.November was also the seventh consecutive month of decline since the USunemployment rate reached 14.7% in April.

制造业降温趋势突显！美国10月工厂和耐用品订单环比涨幅均逊于前值

The trend of manufacturingcooling is highlighted! The month-on-month increase in US factory and durablegoods orders was lower than the previous value

10月份，美国工厂订单与耐用品订单都连续第六个月增长，也都弱于9月的环比涨势。周二公布的美国11月ISM制造业指数脱离两年高位，显示出随着新冠疫情在秋冬季复燃，美国制造业已经蒙上阴影。
In October, both US factory orders anddurable goods orders increased for the sixth consecutive month, which were alsoweaker than the month-on-month increase in September. The US November ISMmanufacturing index released on Tuesday broke from a two-year high, showingthat as the new crown epidemic resumes in autumn and winter, the USmanufacturing industry has been overshadowed.

拜登：非农就业太差赶紧行动 本月和下月应各来一次刺激案

Biden: Non-agriculturalemployment is too poor, act quickly

拜登说，新刺激案可能包括给家庭直接发1200美元。国会民主党和共和党领袖均希望，将刺激计划和避免本月政府关门的综合性开支协议绑在一起。这增加了年底达成协议的可能。
Biden said the new stimulus case mayinclude a direct payment of $1,200 to the family. The leaders of both Democratsand Republicans in Congress hope to tie the stimulus plan to a comprehensivespending agreement that avoids this month’s government shutdown. This increasesthe likelihood of reaching an agreement at the end of the year.

大量疫苗上市在望：辉瑞疫苗接近今年5000万剂目标 Moderna预计一季度1亿剂

A large number of vaccines are onthe horizon: Pfizer vaccine is close to the target of 50 million doses thisyear, Moderna expects 100 million doses in the first quarter

辉瑞合作方BioNTech称，已完成今年的大部分生产目标。Moderna预计明年可产5亿剂。中国专家称，年内将有6亿支国产灭活疫苗获批上市。
BioNTech, a Pfizer partner, said that mostof this year’s production goals have been completed. Moderna expects to produce500 million doses next year. According to Chinese experts, 600 milliondomestically-made inactivated vaccines will be approved for marketing withinthis year.

波音考虑出售股权 再度削减787产量 股价跌超2%

Boeing considersselling equity to cut 787 production again, stock price drops more than 2%

波音公司首席财务官格雷格·史密斯周五表示，由于新冠病毒大流行引起国际旅行需求下降，波音公司将其787 Dreamliner的月产量目标从六架进一步下调至五架。此外，波音正在研究股票出售和其他减轻债务负担的方法，今年以来该公司的债务已飙升至610亿美元。
Boeing Chief Financial Officer Greg Smithsaid on Friday that due to the decline in international travel demand caused bythe new crown virus pandemic, Boeing further lowered its monthly productiontarget for the 787 Dreamliner from six to five. In addition, Boeing is studyingstock sales and other ways to reduce the debt burden. Since the beginning ofthis year, the company’s debt has soared to 61 billion US dollars.

疫苗点燃复苏预期 通胀交易持续升温

Vaccines ignite recoveryexpectations, inflation trading continues to heat up

强劲、快速的经济反弹，再加上美联储表示愿意让通货膨胀率持续维持在2％以上的目标，令投资者担心，未来通货膨胀会侵蚀本来就很低的债券收益率的实际价值。
The strong and rapid economic rebound,coupled with the Fed’s stated willingness to keep the inflation rate above 2%,has made investors worry that future inflation will erode the real value of thealready low bond yields.

英国脱欧谈判暂停 英镑短线下挫

Brexit talks suspended, poundslumps short-term

英国和欧盟的退欧谈判代表在推特上发布联合声明称，双方在公平竞争、治理和渔业方面的重大分歧，未能满足协议的条件。在此基础上，双方同意暂停会谈，以便向其负责人介绍谈判的进展情况。欧盟主席冯德莱恩和英国总理约翰逊将在明天下午讨论英国脱欧的局势。周五，英镑兑美元短线下挫大约20点，最低触及1.3457。
The Brexit negotiators of the UnitedKingdom and the European Union issued a joint statement on Twitter stating thatthe two sides have failed to meet the conditions of the agreement on majordifferences in fair competition, governance and fisheries. On this basis, thetwo parties agreed to suspend the talks in order to brief their leaders on theprogress of the negotiations. EU President Von der Lein and British PrimeMinister Johnson will discuss the situation of Brexit tomorrow afternoon. On Friday,the British pound fell about 20 points against the U.S. dollar short-term,reaching a minimum of 1.3457.

欧洲“印钞机”更凶猛：欧央行下周扩大刺激“板上钉钉”
