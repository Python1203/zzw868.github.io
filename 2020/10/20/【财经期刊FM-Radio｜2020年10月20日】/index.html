要闻详情

4.9%！中国三季度GDP同比增速加快 4.9%! China’s third quarter GDP growth rate accelerated year-on-year

前三季度经济增长由负转正，同比增长0.7%。分季度看，在一季度同比下降6.8%、二季度同比增长3.2%的基础上，三季度增速加快至4.9%，呈现稳定恢复增长态势。
Economic growth in the first three quarters turned from negative to positive, with a year-on-year increase of 0.7%. In quarterly terms, on the basis of a year-on-year decline of 6.8% in the first quarter and a year-on-year growth of 3.2% in the second quarter, the growth rate in the third quarter accelerated to 4.9%, showing a steady recovery growth trend.

年内新高！中国9月规模以上工业增加值同比增长6.9%New high during the year! China’s industrial added value above designated size in September increased by 6.9%

前三季度，全国规模以上工业增加值同比增长1.2%，上半年为下降1.3%。其中，三季度同比增长5.8%。
In the first three quarters, the value added of the industrial enterprises above designated size nationwide increased by 1.2% year-on-year, and fell by 1.3% in the first half of the year. Among them, the third quarter increased by 5.8% year-on-year.

增幅显著扩大！中国9月社会消费品零售总额同比增3.3%The increase has expanded significantly! China’s total retail sales of consumer goods in September increased 3.3% year-on-year

中国9月社会消费品零售总额连续第二个月实现正增长，且增幅较上月继续扩大，汽车销售继续增长，饮料烟酒、化妆品增速最高，服装增速继续加快。
China’s total retail sales of consumer goods in September achieved positive growth for the second consecutive month, and the growth rate continued to expand from the previous month. Auto sales continued to grow, beverages, tobacco, alcohol, and cosmetics grew at the highest rate, and apparel growth continued to accelerate.

连续7个月上行！中国1-9月房地产开发投资同比增长5.6%Upward for 7 consecutive months! China’s real estate development investment increased by 5.6% from January to September

1-9月份，全国房地产开发投资103484亿元，同比增长5.6%，增速比1-8月份提高1.0个百分点，也高于市场预期的5.2%。From January to September, the national real estate development investment was 10,348.4 billion yuan, a year-on-year increase of 5.6%, and the growth rate was 1.0 percentage point higher than that from January to August, and was also higher than the market’s expected 5.2%.

由降转增！中国1-9月城镇固定资产投资同比增长0.8%From down to increase! China’s urban fixed asset investment increased by 0.8% from January to September

这是城镇固定资产投资增速年内首次转为正增长。
This is the first time the urban fixed asset investment growth rate has turned positive during the year.

证监会官网显示，蚂蚁集团已于10月16日收到证监会有关其申请赴港上市的行政许可决定书，并于10月19日接收了书面回复。According to the official website of the China Securities Regulatory Commission, Ant Group has received the China Securities Regulatory Commission’s administrative license decision on its application for listing in Hong Kong on October 16, and received a written response on October 19.

媒体称，蚂蚁集团香港IPO已获得中国证监会批准。此外，有消息称蚂蚁集团香港IPO获得港交所批准。
According to the media, the Hong Kong IPO of Ant Group has been approved by the China Securities Regulatory Commission. In addition, it is reported that the Hong Kong IPO of Ant Group has been approved by the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.

媒体：深圳部分大行收紧“房抵贷”，住宅类放缓、商铺等基本停贷。

Media: Some major banks in Shenzhen have tightened their “mortgage loans”, residential housing has slowed down, and commercial shops have basically stopped lending.

阿里加码同城零售！280亿港元控股大润发母公司，实现并表Ali overweights retail in the same city! 28 billion Hong Kong dollars to control RT-Mart’s parent company, and achieve consolidation

在完成股份购买后，阿里还将对高鑫剩余股份发出全面收购要约，可能额外支付最多约170亿港元。同城零售事业群成为今年阿里新的一号工程，而大润发则是其供给侧的核心。
After the completion of the share purchase, Ali will also issue a general takeover offer for the remaining shares of Gaoxin, possibly paying an additional up to approximately HK$17 billion. The same city retail business group has become Ali’s new No. 1 project this year, and RT-Mart is the core of its supply side.

“二师兄”有多赚钱？龙头牧原股份前三季度暴赚200多亿，同比狂飙1413%How much money does “Second Brother” make? The leader Muyuan made more than 20 billion in the first three quarters, a surge of 1413% year-on-year

周一牧原股份披露第三季报，今年前三季度净利润210亿，同比增长1413.28%！日赚一个亿，卖猪肉就有这么赚钱。
On Monday, Muyuan shares disclosed the third quarter report, the net profit of the first three quarters of this year was 21 billion, a year-on-year increase of 1413.28%! If you earn 100 million a day, you can make money selling pork.

国产特斯拉整车出口海外，首批Model 3预计下周发货Domestically produced Tesla vehicles are exported overseas, and the first batch of Model 3 is expected to be shipped next week

首批出口的车辆为Model 3标准续航升级版，与供应中国市场的车型保持一致。
The first batch of exported vehicles is an upgraded version of the Model 3 standard battery life, which is consistent with the models supplied to the Chinese market.

11月初以前能否出台新刺激在此一举！佩洛西与姆努钦据称有进展 周二继续对话Whether or not a new stimulus can be introduced before early November is here! Pelosi and Mnuchin reportedly made progress and continue their dialogue on Tuesday

众议院民主党领袖佩洛西此前表示，周二结束前的谈判将决定，11月大选前能否出台新刺激。佩洛西的发言人称，周一与财长姆努钦通话后，双方的分歧继续减少。
House Democratic leader Pelosi previously said that negotiations before the end of Tuesday will determine whether new stimulus will be introduced before the November election. A spokesman for Pelosi said that after the phone call with Finance Minister Mnuchin on Monday, the differences between the two sides continued to decrease.

鲍威尔：美联储尚未决定发行数字货币Powell: The Fed has not yet decided to issue digital currency

Fed Chairman Powell stated that the Fed has assessed the benefits of digital currencies; it will handle this issue very carefully.

“纳斯达克鲸”卷土重来？软银股票交易部门据称持仓逾200亿美元”Nasdaq Whale” makes a comeback? SoftBank’s stock trading department reportedly held more than $20 billion

若消息属实，软银的股票持仓可能已远超目标水平，今年8月爆出的软银目标是逾100亿美元。媒体还称，上周一纳斯达克100创4月以来最大涨幅当天，有交易者认购约2亿美元的科技股看涨期权。同日，软银股价创出二十年新高，处于2000年科技泡沫以来最高水平；公司创始人孙正义此前放弃激进投资策略，大规模出售资产、削减公司债务水平。
If the news is true, SoftBank’s stock holdings may have far exceeded the target level. The SoftBank target that broke out in August this year was more than 10 billion US dollars. The media also said that on the day of the Nasdaq 100’s biggest increase since April last Monday, traders subscribed for call options on technology stocks worth about $200 million. On the same day, Softbank’s stock price hit a new high in 20 years, at the highest level since the technology bubble in 2000; the company’s founder, Sun Zhengyi, had previously abandoned his aggressive investment strategy, sold assets on a large scale and reduced the company’s debt level.

价值近100亿美元的页岩油气并购！康菲将溢价15%收购康乔A shale oil and gas acquisition worth nearly $10 billion! ConocoPhillips will acquire Concho at a premium of 15%

康菲（ConocoPhillips）同意收购康乔（Concho Resources）总价值97亿美元的股票，这将是今年页岩油气行业最大的一笔并购交易，同时也将打造出一家新的美国页岩油开采巨头。合并后的公司价值将会达到600亿美元，产量将能直接与美孚、西方石油等巨头抗衡。
ConocoPhillips agreed to acquire Concho Resources worth US$9.7 billion. This will be the largest M&A transaction in the shale oil and gas industry this year. It will also create a new American shale oil mining giant . The combined company’s value will reach 60 billion U.S. dollars, and its output will be able to directly compete with giants such as Mobil and Western Petroleum.

英特尔一度涨近4% 接近以约100亿美元出售NAND闪存业务Intel rose nearly 4% at one time, close to selling NAND flash memory business for about $10 billion

媒体称，英特尔接近达成协议，将出售NAND芯片业务给韩国SK海力士。英特尔股价由跌转涨，日高涨3.8%，闪存领域的竞争对手西部数据和美光科技也短线飙升。富国银行称，英特尔的出售决定将利好于西部数据。
According to media, Intel is close to reaching an agreement to sell its NAND chip business to SK Hynix of South Korea. Intel’s stock price turned from a decline to a rise, rising 3.8% daily, and rivals Western Digital and Micron Technology in the flash memory field also soared in the short term. Wells Fargo said that Intel’s decision to sell will benefit Western Digital.

美国房屋建筑商信心连续两个月创新高 劳动力和建材短缺成风险U.S. homebuilders’ confidence hits a new high for two consecutive months, labor and building materials shortages are at risk

美国房屋建筑商信心指数在9月和10月首次连续两个月突破80整数位，也是连续六个月超过市场预期。但缺少劳动力、土地、建筑材料和新一轮财政刺激协议，都可能减缓美国楼市的发展动能。
The U.S. Home Builders Confidence Index exceeded 80 in September and October for the first time for two consecutive months, which also exceeded market expectations for six consecutive months. However, the lack of labor, land, building materials, and a new round of fiscal stimulus agreements may slow down the development momentum of the US housing market.

2、特斯拉 | 据报道，特斯拉上海超级工厂已于今年10月启动整车出口业务，出口国家包括德国、法国、意大利、西班牙等。首批出口的车辆为Model3标准续航升级版，与供应中国市场的车型保持一致，现已陆续运抵上海海通国际汽车码头，预计下周二启航。

特斯拉上海超级工厂现已基本完成Model3的产能爬坡，后续项目一期二阶段正在建设，建成后将形成25-30万辆/年纯电动乘用车生产能力。华泰证券刘千琳指出，部分中国汽车零配件供应商将持续受益于特斯拉汽车销量的高增长，以及特斯拉在中国的产能扩张和新产品投放。旭升股份为特斯拉提供铝压铸零部件，最新公告前三季度实现净利润2.31亿元，同比增长70%；拓普集团是特斯拉底盘以及轻量化结构件的核心供应商。

3、eSIM | 工信部19日发布批复文件，同意中国移动、中国电信开展物联网等领域eSIM技术应用服务。服务仅限开通数据业务和与之相关的定向话音、定向短信业务。

eSIM卡可以集成在设备终端的芯片上，无须插入运营商单独发行的SIM卡，成本更低且更稳定。华泰证券王林认为，eSIM的发展与智能终端的小型化、功能稳定性等要求密切相关，也是运营商拓展新业务的重要抓手。此外，对于智能手表等可穿戴设备厂商而言，产品渗透率有望借机加速提升。紫光国微已与联通华盛签署战略合作协议，就eSIM业务展开合作；环旭电子是苹果手表的独家供应商，该产品支持eSIM功能；东信和平研发的eSIM集成化模块能为物联网终端打造身份标签。
